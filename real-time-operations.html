1SecondCopy

AI-Augmented Content Agency, launched 2021

Outcomes
  • 90% of the pipeline automated end-to-end
  • Headcount reduced from 12 contractors to fewer than 3 operators
  • Lead response time dropped from 6 hours to under 10 minutes
  • Content turnaround cut from 5 days to 24 hours
Challenge

1SecondCopy was scaling fast but bleeding margin on every project. Each piece of content required manual lead qualification, back-and-forth briefing, freelancer assignment, QA review, and client delivery—all coordinated across Slack, email, and spreadsheets. The founder needed to either hire more people or find a way to make three operators do the work of twelve.

Solution
  • Built an AI cold-email engine that qualifies inbound leads, scores intent, and drafts personalised follow-ups automatically
  • Created an ops pipeline that ingests client briefs, generates first-draft content with AI, and routes it through a human QA layer before delivery
  • Integrated the full workflow with their CRM so every touchpoint—from first email to final invoice—is tracked without manual data entry
  • Deployed real-time dashboards so leadership can monitor pipeline health, content velocity, and revenue per operator at a glance
